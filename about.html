Hello,


I am currently studying graphic design and photography at Oregon State University. I work for the Linus Pauling Institute as a graphic designer, and for University Housing & Dining Services as a marketing and design assistant. I create flashcards, posters, signs, icons, digital slides, scientific figures and many other kinds of media! Additionally, I have a bachelor’s degree in psychology and in art from the University of Nevada, Reno.

Outside of school and work I love playing tabletop and video games, chillin’ with my cats, and eating at great restaurants.

Thanks for stopping by and feel free to contact me!
