"Supercat"

A comic inspired three image series depicting Oliver* as Supercat! This work resulted from taking a step out of my comfort zone by creating something that's only meant to be ridiculous and silly.

High saturation is present throughout the images to invoke a more comic book and super hero like feeling.

*Oliver is my own cat and his safety was the number one priority. He was not harmed in the making of these images. He also had a lot of fun playing with that stick.
