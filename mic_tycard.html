"MIC Thank You Card"

The Micronutrient Information Center (MIC) is a subsidiary of the Linus Pauling Institute. This thank you card is shipped out to people who have donated to the MIC.

The actual card has a high resolution hand-written digital signature after "Warm regards,". It was removed from the online representation upon request.
